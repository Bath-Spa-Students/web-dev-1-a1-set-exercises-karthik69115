Movie Review

"Life Of Pie" is a interesting film which consist of fantasy and adventure. In this movies the main character Pie and his family go in a ship to canada as his fathers' zoo breakes. So his father ships all the valuable animals and his family in a cargo ship. But unluckily an unexpected storm occurs and the ship sinks. Pi finds himself in a life boat with a Bengal Tiger. This movie was super hit on 2012 s'. They sold more than 12 million copies by 2015.
