Plot

Life Of Pie is a adventures thriller movie released on december 20, 2012. The movie was directed by well known director Ang Lee. Life Of Pie movie is based on a novel of the same name written by Yann Martel which was released on 2001. The story is based on two survivers of a ship wreck who survived 227 days in the pacific ocean. One is bengal tiger and other is 16 year old boy named Pi Patel. In this movie Pi s' father santosh patel had a zoo in Pondicherry, India. Unluckily they had financial issues which ended up in shutting the zoo. Pi and his family planned to go to canada with some of their precious animals. Unexpectedly a huge storm occurs and their ship wrecked; some how pi survived the storm and found himself in a lifeboat. In the boat he realised that life is uncertain as he conforont hyna (cook). All at a sudden, there comes the central character a bengal tiger (richard parker) approached and attacked the hyna. It was a game of "survival of the fittest". Now Pi and Richard left in the boat and both wishes to lead a life.

So his father ships all the valuable animals and his family in a cargo ship. But unluckily an unexpected storm occurs and the ship sinks. Pi finds himself in a life boat with a Bengal Tiger.
